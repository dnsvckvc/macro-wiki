# Invert, Always Invert

A reasoning heuristic popularized by [[CharlieMunger]] (borrowed from the mathematician Carl Jacobi): solve hard problems by asking the opposite question. Instead of "how do I succeed?", ask "what would cause failure?" and then avoid those conditions.

## Applied in the wiki
[[PauloMacro]] uses the heuristic in [[crack-spreads-are-the-tell]] to read the April 2026 refinery-cracks setup as the mirror image of the April 2020 [[CovidOilCrash2020]]. In 2020, rising [[CrackSpreads]] foreshadowed a crude *crash*; today, falling cracks during a supply-shock regime foreshadow a crude *moon*. Same indicator, opposite sign, opposite outcome — that's the invert trade.
