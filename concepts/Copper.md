# Copper

Industrial base metal; a central [[PauloMacro]] theme via the copper junior mining sector. Paulo treats copper as being in an active M&A / takeout wave, with supply-side scarcity making proven resources on listed juniors attractive acquisition targets for majors.

## Wiki data points
- Paulo's largest copper junior position: [[AldebaranResources]] (ALDE.CN), which on 9 April 2026 reported ~1 km of 0.62% Cu (0.66% CuEq) on infill drilling — reinforcing its attractiveness as a takeout target. ([[economic-data-copper-rollovers-and-more]])
