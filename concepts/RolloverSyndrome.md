# Rollover Syndrome

A rolling-top price-action framework [[PauloMacro]] developed in his "Nifty Fifty and the Rollover" series. Describes the characteristic way once-leading megacap stocks transition from uptrend to downtrend: a slow, stair-step rolling of the top rather than a sharp reversal. The framework includes both a pattern-recognition component and a set of trading rules for playing the rollover.

## Application (April 2026)
Paulo flags [[Amazon]] as being "in an interesting spot" in the Rollover Syndrome framework as of 9 April 2026 ([[economic-data-copper-rollovers-and-more]]). Consistent with — but not dependent on — the broader [[MAG7]] leadership-rotation thesis articulated by [[KevinMuir]] in [[what-not-to-buy]].
