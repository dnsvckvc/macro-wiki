# TRIN (Arms Index)

Also known as the Arms Index. A market-internals indicator comparing the ratio of advancing-to-declining *issues* against the ratio of advancing-to-declining *volume*. Values above 1 indicate relatively more volume in declining stocks (distributive); values below 1 indicate more volume in advancers (accumulative).

## Usage in the wiki
[[PauloMacro]] in [[economic-data-copper-rollovers-and-more]] cites TRIN approaching 2 during an index rally as evidence that the move is distributive, not accumulative: prices are up but heavy volume is flowing into the declining names. Combined with the exit from the [[PreOpexMisdirectionWindow]], he reads this as favouring a near-term reversal.
