# Broken Themes

A trading/positioning concept attributed to [[ArrakisGlobal]] and quoted by [[KevinMuir]]:

> "Being a late convert to a theme is dangerous — you risk getting stuck in 'broken themes' when the market has moved on."

## Idea
Once a winning theme has become consensus and heavily owned, new converts arriving late are the marginal buyer when leadership has already rotated elsewhere. Subsequent returns are poor and exits are crowded. Closely related to the [[BruceKovner]] observation that a heavily-owned story failing to rally on good news is signalling reversal.

## Applied to [[MAG7]]
[[KevinMuir]] argues in [[what-not-to-buy]] that MAG7 risks being the broken theme of this cycle: record retail ownership, worst-performing bucket over six months, and fundamentals (free cash flow) deteriorating under [[AIInfrastructureInvestment]] load.
