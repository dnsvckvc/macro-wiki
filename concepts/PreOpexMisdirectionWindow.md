# Pre-Opex Misdirection Window

A recurring Tuesday–Thursday window roughly **9 days before monthly options expiry** when, per [[PauloMacro]], positioning tends to be "rinsed" in one or two directions as theta and gamma on outstanding options begin to bite and dealers adjust their hedges.

## Signature
- Often visible as a "V", an inverted carrot "^", or an "S" on its side on the tape.
- Sometimes reverses the prevailing trend outright; sometimes is merely a "cha-cha-cha" before it resumes.
- "Turnaround Tuesday" frequently shows up inside this window.
- Not a fixed mechanical rule — a window where "weird stuff can happen."

## Application (April 2026)
Per [[economic-data-copper-rollovers-and-more]], the market was *exiting* this window on 9 April 2026. Combined with a new high being made inside the window and distributive internals ([[TRIN]] approaching 2), Paulo reads the setup as favouring a turn back down.
