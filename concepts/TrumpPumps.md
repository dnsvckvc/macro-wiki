# Trump Pumps

[[PauloMacro]]'s shorthand for [[DonaldTrump]]'s episodic attempts to jam markets higher via jawboning — tweets, statements, after-hours announcements. Per [[jumping-the-shark-again]] (27 March 2026), the pumps are decaying in both **amplitude** and **wavelength**:

- Amplitude: reduced to ~30 SPX handles on the most recent pump.
- Wavelength: the interval between effective pumps is shortening.
- Execution: Trump has been resorting to the 4:15pm post-close window when futures are thin and easier to move ("must be learning from a true market pro").
- Paulo's tease: "Can't we get a 'pretty complete' 2% anymore?"

The decay is read as a tell that the physical-world reality is grinding the rhetorical tools to zero effect — setup for a [[TomHanksHasCovidMoment]].

## TACO decay timeline
- **Liberation Day TACO** (~April 2025): SPX was down −20% from its Feb 19th high. TACO produced a **+10% rally**. This worked because the crisis was Trump's own creation and he undid it by reversing his own policy.
- **Iran "Mission Accomplished" TACO** (10 March 2026): SPX was down only −3% from ATH. TACO produced **+83bps**. This one is structurally different: the war is not a single-player game and Iran's optimal strategy is a [[NashEquilibrium]] — Trump cannot unilaterally resolve it.
- Per Paulo ([[jumping-the-shark-when-a-taco-is-not-a-taco]]): *"Commodities are not a cartoon."* Trump has "singlehandedly made the crude futures market 'untradable' — $40/barrel daily range — and probably blew up every producer's hedge book."
