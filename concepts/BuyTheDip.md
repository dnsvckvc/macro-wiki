# Buy the Dip

Reflexive playbook of adding to winners after a drawdown, justified by past cycles where the same trade worked. Particularly dominant in post-COVID [[MAG7]] positioning.

## Where it breaks down — per [[what-not-to-buy]]
[[KevinMuir]] argues the sell-side's April 2026 "buy the MAG7 dip" recommendation fails on three counts:
1. **Leadership has rotated.** MAG7 is the worst-performing bucket over the trailing six months — you're chasing the laggard.
2. **Ownership is extended.** Record public MAG7 holdings mean there's little marginal buyer left — a [[BrokenThemes]] / [[BruceKovner]] setup.
3. **The fundamental prop is weakening.** [[AIInfrastructureInvestment]] is crushing FCF; on [[PriceToFreeCashFlow]] MAG7 has never been more expensive.

The implication: "buy the dip" is strongest when a winner corrects *while still leading*, not after leadership has already shifted.
