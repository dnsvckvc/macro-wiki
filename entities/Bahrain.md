# Bahrain

Small GCC monarchy and oil producer — home to the US Fifth Fleet at Naval Support Activity Bahrain, the most important US naval base in the Middle East. In the April 2026 Iran crisis, Bahrain is notable more for its geostrategic role than its production footprint.

## April 2026 position (per [[wctw-the-oil-market-breaking-point]])
- **−0.18mmbpd of production shut in** — the smallest absolute contributor to [[HFIResearch]]'s 10.98mmbpd GCC shut-in total, but included for completeness.
- Fifth Fleet Bahrain was evacuated of 1,500 people during the crisis per [[LukeGromen]] in [[macrovoices-528-luke-gromen]] — an indicator that the US itself regarded the threat environment as severe enough to pull non-combat personnel.
