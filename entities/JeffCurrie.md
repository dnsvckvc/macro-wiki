# Jeff Currie

Veteran commodity strategist (formerly Goldman Sachs head of commodities research; subsequently Carlyle). Cited indirectly in [[macrovoices-529-ole-hansen]] for two of the wiki's load-bearing super-cycle frames:

- **[[JustInTimeVsJustInCase]]** — the post-2020 regime change in global supply chains: economies move from minimal inventory to *insurance-grade* stockpiles, structurally lifting commodity demand.
- **"The old world is striking back against the new world"** — the energy-transition friction frame: legacy energy/materials supply (the "old world") cannot keep up with the demand growth from electrification and AI compute (the "new world"), so prices clear via shortage rather than via investment response.

Both frames are core to [[OleHansen]]'s third-wave [[CommoditySupercycle]] thesis.
