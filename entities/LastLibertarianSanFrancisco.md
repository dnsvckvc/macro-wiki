# The Last Libertarian in San Francisco

[[KevinMuir]]'s pseudonym for an anonymous friend who is "not allowed (or doesn't want to be) in the spotlight." Muir notes he has referenced this same friend before and likely will again — Muir credits him as a recurring source of sharp one-liners that cut through consensus.

## Wiki reference — the gold one-liner
Source of the load-bearing one-liner in [[geopolitics-doesnt-change-prices-in-the-long-run]] (12 March 2026):

> *"Everyone loves to say that geopolitics doesn't move asset prices, but that's just wrong. Gold over the past four years would have something to say about that!"*

Muir: *"I instantly realized he was so RIGHT."* The line became the scaffolding for Muir's pushback against the over-applied [[FadeGeopoliticalAngst]] framework during the Iran/US/Israel war — the *"transformative-event exception"* that the [[LedgerToRealWorldThesis]] in gold 2022–2025 empirically demonstrated.
