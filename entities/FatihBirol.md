# Fatih Birol

Executive Director of the **[[IEA]]** (International Energy Agency). Globally-visible spokesperson for energy policy; routinely quoted in financial media on oil-market conditions.

## Referenced in the wiki for
Per [[AdiImsirovic]] in [[oilgroundup-adi-imsirovic]], Birol made a public statement during the crisis that futures markets "will converge with physical markets." Imsirovic's critique:
- Futures *always* converge with physical on delivery month, because futures become physical. That's the definition of the contract.
- What the statement fails to explain is that **Dated Brent stays above the futures strip** even after convergence, because the *next* month's Dated takes over. The backwardation just shifts forward.
- Imsirovic: *"I don't know whether he understands it or not… confused the hell out of everyone."*

The incident illustrates how even the most senior energy-policy figures can make microstructure claims that are technically inaccurate or misleading — a warning sign for how political actors may interpret the market during the crisis.
