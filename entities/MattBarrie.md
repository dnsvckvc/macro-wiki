# Matt Barrie

CEO and founder of **Freelancer.com** (ASX: FLN), the world's largest cloud workforce marketplace (~87 million users). Stanford EE degree. Australian tech entrepreneur. Was in Silicon Valley during the 1997-2000 dot-com boom and bust. The wiki's **sixth author** and first voice from *inside* the tech industry.

Barrie provides the production-side evidence for what the wiki's financial authors observe from the outside:
