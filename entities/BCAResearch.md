# BCA Research

Independent global macro research firm, Montreal-based. 77 years old as of 2026 — founded in **1949 by Hamilton Bolton**, stewarded for decades by **Tony Boeckh**.

BCA = **Bank Credit Analyst** (the original name). [[MarkoPapic]] in [[my-buddy-marko-broke-the-market]]: *"Like none of us are doing bank credit analysis anymore."* The firm has continuously re-anchored its research product: under former CEO Bashar Al-Rahani it launched the geopolitical service in 2011 (brought on Papic to run it; today run by Matt Kirk). Papic runs the firm's **geo-macro** synthesis — the combined macro + geopolitics franchise.

## Wiki relevance
- Employer and platform of [[MarkoPapic]], whose [[GeopoliticalAlpha]] / [[FadeGeopoliticalAngst]] framework is cited repeatedly in wiki sources.
- BCA's January 6, 2026 long-Brent recommendation (via Papic's client service) is the **earliest institutional long-oil call in the wiki**, predating [[PauloMacro]]'s 28 January [[oil-has-turned-a-very-big-corner]] by three weeks.
