# Le Shrub

Macro Substack writer and friend of [[PauloMacro]]. Described by Paulo as "undefeated on memes."

## Contributions to the wiki
- **[[ShrubsRazor]]** — Le Shrub's philosophical observation that "once you realize it's all nonsense, it starts to make sense" was elevated by Paulo into a formal principle: *"The funniest, most absurd outcome is also the most likely one."* Introduced in [[oil-has-turned-a-very-big-corner]] (28 January 2026). Applied: Trump banning US crude exports.
- **NACHO** — coined "Not Actually Changing Hormuz Opening" in late March 2026, riffing on the TACO meme. See [[NachoTaco]]. ([[jumping-the-shark-again]])
- **Wheat trade** — discussed the wheat-as-geopolitical-VIX thesis with Paulo. See [[Wheat]]. ([[make-1973-great-again]])
