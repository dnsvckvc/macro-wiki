# HFI Research

Independent oil research shop whose work [[PauloMacro]] relies on for physical-market data and OPEC spare capacity analysis. Uses Kpler satellite/shipping data.

## Referenced in the wiki for
In [[oil-has-turned-a-very-big-corner]], Paulo cites HFI for:
- **Global onshore crude inventory analysis** — most of the floating barrels land in China; ex-China global onshore stocks only up ~70mmbbl YoY despite the IEA's surplus narrative.
- **OPEC+ spare capacity audit** (December 2025) — 17 of 22 OPEC+ countries achieved peak production *before* Covid; only Gabon, Libya, UAE, and Kazakhstan are producing above their 2013–25 peaks. Real spare capacity is far less than the consensus 3–5mmbpd; HFI estimates Saudi peaks around 11mmbpd. This audit is central to Paulo's argument that the market is sleeping on a structural supply shortfall.
