Notable Trade-of-the-Week structure from [[macrovoices-529-ole-hansen]] — explicitly designed to monetize [[RollYield]] / backwardation in deferred crude:

- **Bull call spread on Dec 2026 WTI** using November 2026 options (208 days to expiry).
- Buy $70 call ($11.70, of which $7.40 is intrinsic) / sell $90 call ($4.40).
- Net debit $7.30; spread width $20; break-even ≈ $77.30 (≈ underlying).
- Max loss $7.30; max profit $12.70.
- Premium "largely intrinsic value" → low vega, minimal time decay → defined-risk exposure to the deferred curve rather than front-month volatility.
