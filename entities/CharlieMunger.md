# Charlie Munger

Vice chairman of Berkshire Hathaway and longtime intellectual partner to Warren Buffett. Referenced in the wiki as the source of the [[InvertAlwaysInvert]] heuristic — approach a problem by asking what would cause the *opposite* outcome, then avoid those conditions.

## Where he shows up in the wiki
- [[PauloMacro]] invokes "invert, always invert" in [[crack-spreads-are-the-tell]] to reason about refinery cracks mirroring April 2020 but in the opposite direction.
