# Avis Budget Car Rental — CAR

Rental-car company and Dow Jones Transports component. Referenced in the wiki as the **April 2026 poster child for 2021-style retail-led meme squeezes** resurfacing inside the post-JPM-roll rally.

## Wiki reference
Per [[KevinMuir]] in [[making-sense-of-the-last-two-months]] (19 April 2026):
- AVIS began rallying towards end of March; the rally became "manic" starting **1 April 2026.**
- **Dow Jones Transports rallied +23.37% from the 30 March 2026 low.**
- **Over half of that rise was AVIS alone.**

Muir reads this as early evidence that "during the first two weeks of the rally, the market was already showing signs of trading in a 2021 fashion, where retail-led squeezes dominated." The squeeze was a warning signal under the hood even as the headline indices looked ordinary.
