# Robert Pape

Professor of political science at the University of Chicago. Author of *Bombing to Win* (on the effectiveness of strategic bombing) and *Dying to Win* (on the logic of suicide terrorism). A leading scholar on coercive strategies and asymmetric conflict.

## Referenced in the wiki for
[[PauloMacro]] in [[the-north-star-vs-liquidity]] cites Pape's **Escalation Trap** framework as independent academic confirmation of his [[NorthStar]]. Pape has been calling for the "exact sequencing we are now seeing in US-Iranian relations" via five stages. His latest:

> *"Iran does not need to win militarily. It needs to sustain disruption at a level that forces the global economy to adjust under constraint."*

Paulo: "Imagine my pleasant surprise when professor Robert Pape gave an outstanding interview two weeks ago where he laid out his Escalation Trap that just happens to back into my North Star perfectly."
