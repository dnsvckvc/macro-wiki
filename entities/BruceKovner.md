# Bruce Kovner

Legendary macro trader, founder of Caxton Associates. Referenced in the wiki as the source of two related positioning-framework quotes.

## Frameworks referenced in wiki
- **"Over-owned story failing to rally" (via [[what-not-to-buy]]):** a market that *should* rally on bullish news/positioning but doesn't is sending a warning. When a story is well-known and heavily owned yet prices fail to advance, the path of least resistance is likely down. [[KevinMuir]] invokes this framework to argue against buying the [[MAG7]] dip.
- **"Consensus the market is not confirming" (via [[oil-and-retail]]):** *"What I am really looking for is a consensus the market is not confirming. I like to know that there are a lot of people who are going to be wrong."* [[PauloMacro]] applies this to the retail oil-short positioning in [[SCO]] (~$1bn AUM of 2x inverse WTI) and [[USO]] short interest (10.7mn shares = $1.4bn notional at 7.9% borrow) — weak hands on the wrong side of a structurally bullish crude setup.

The two applications are symmetrical: one uses it for equities (MAG7) where the consensus is long and the market isn't rallying; the other for oil where the consensus (retail) is short and the market isn't cratering. Both are contrarian reads on a refusing tape.
