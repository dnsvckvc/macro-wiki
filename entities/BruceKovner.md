# Bruce Kovner

Legendary macro trader, founder of Caxton Associates. Referenced in the wiki primarily as a source of trading wisdom on sentiment and positioning.

## Framework referenced in wiki
A market that *should* rally on bullish news/positioning but doesn't is sending a warning. When a story is well-known and heavily owned yet prices fail to advance, the path of least resistance is likely down. [[KevinMuir]] invokes this framework in [[what-not-to-buy]] to argue against buying the [[MAG7]] dip.
