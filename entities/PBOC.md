# People's Bank of China (PBOC)

China's central bank. Wiki interest is focused on its role as a structural buyer of [[Gold]] as part of the [[LedgerToRealWorldThesis]] reserve-diversification trend.

## Muir's model of PBOC behavior ([[buying-gold-again]])
- **Objective:** maximize the *total quantity* of gold accumulated over the next decade, not minimize average entry price or chase momentum.
- **Implication:** the PBOC is a price-insensitive buyer on weakness and a *voluntarily absent* buyer on strength.
- **Muir's read of the 2026 correction:** during the speculative mania into the Middle East war the PBOC "completely walked away from the gold market" to avoid pushing price further. Now that the 27% correction has shaken out late speculators, they are expected to resume buying.
- *"It would be imprudent for them to not diversify into gold."*
- Someday — years out — the PBOC will own enough gold that it no longer provides a tailwind. Until then, Muir wants to own what they are buying.

## Howell's PBoC-as-engine framing ([[crypto-gold-pboc-vs-fed]], 27 April 2026)

[[MichaelHowell]] elevates the PBoC from "structural buyer of gold" to **the second of three engines of Global Liquidity** alongside the [[ReserveManagementPurchases|US Fed]] and the [[MOVEIndex]]. As of April 2026, the PBoC engine is **stalling** — its expected aggressive easing (driven by Trump tariff pressure + China's debt overhang) has paused, *"possibly the uncertainty over Iran has caused the Chinese authorities to pause, reflect and rein back its stimulus."*

**The PBoC-Gold causal mechanism:** Howell publishes the explicit chart of *cumulative PBoC liquidity injections* against the bullion price — *"a clear cause and effect from the PBoC to the bullion price."*

**Implication for the gold consolidation:** the wiki now has **three independent attributions** for the gold pause through April 2026:
- [[KevinMuir]] ([[buying-gold-again]]): positioning unwind — speculators were crowded long, Gulf cash calls + Turkey wartime selling forced de-grossing; PBoC walks away during the mania, expected to resume.
- [[OleHansen]] ([[macrovoices-529-ole-hansen]]): technical — 200-day moving average held at the 50% retracement; pattern-matches Liberation Day, dot-com, GFC.
- **Howell ([[crypto-gold-pboc-vs-fed]]): policy — the PBoC has actively stalled its broader easing program**, removing the cumulative-liquidity-injection tailwind that has driven gold for the cycle.

The three explanations are complementary, not competing. Muir explains the *correction shape*, Hansen the *technical floor*, Howell the *consolidation duration*.
