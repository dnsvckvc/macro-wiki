# Michael Cembalest

Chairman of Market and Investment Strategy at J.P. Morgan Private Bank (previously CIO). Longtime author of the widely-read **"Eye on the Market"** research series. Respected across the hedge fund and asset-management community for data-rich, historically grounded macro pieces.

## Referenced in the wiki for
[[KevinMuir]] in [[we-need-to-talk-about-interest-rates-and-oil]] cites a Cembalest chart from *"Pandora's Bog"* (Eye on the Market, April 2026) that catalogs the historical relationship between oil price spikes and major S&P 500 tops — specifically highlighting that both the 2000 DotCom top (oil up **245%** from 1999–2001) and the 2007–2008 top were accompanied by massive oil rises, ahead of 49% and 56% equity drawdowns respectively.
