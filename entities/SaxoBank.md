# Saxo Bank

Danish online trading bank; publishes commodity research authored by [[OleHansen]] (Head of Commodity Strategy, 18 years).

Distribution channels mentioned in [[macrovoices-529-ole-hansen]]:
- Saxo platforms (clients)
- `home.saxo.com/insights` (public webpage)
- Hansen's daily commodity podcast
- Hansen's CoT (Commitment of Traders) report analysis — described by Eric Townsend as *"the best analysis there is"*

The wiki's first commercial-bank source on commodities; contrasts with the independent-research voices ([[HFIResearch]], [[RoryJohnston]] (Commodity Context), [[MichaelHowell]] (CrossBorder Capital), [[AdamRozencwajg]] (Goehring & Rozencwajg)) and the buy-side voices ([[PauloMacro]], [[KevinMuir]]).
