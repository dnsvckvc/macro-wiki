# Harley Bassman

Veteran rates and options strategist; creator of the **MOVE index** (the Treasury vol equivalent of the VIX) during his time at Merrill Lynch. Now publishes commentary as "The Convexity Maven."

## Referenced in the wiki for
The **TUA ETF** — a product he designed that levers US 2Y Treasury notes to match the duration of the 10Y. [[KevinMuir]] recommends TUA in [[vix-inversion-buying-bonds-japan-jpm-option-whale]] as the retail-friendly way to express his long fixed-income trade, noting that for the first time in a while it **carries positively**.
