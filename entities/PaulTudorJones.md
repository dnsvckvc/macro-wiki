# Paul Tudor Jones

Legendary macro trader and founder of Tudor Investment Corporation. Most famous for his correct call on the 1987 Black Monday crash.

## Referenced in the wiki for
[[KevinMuir]] in [[vix-inversion-buying-bonds-japan-jpm-option-whale]] cites Jones as the historical template for owning duration into a crash: PTJ famously held *tons of 5-year T-notes* through the 1987 crash, which paid off massively as the Fed cut rates in response. Muir uses this precedent to justify his own long 2Y/5Y position in March 2026 now that the front end carries positively.
