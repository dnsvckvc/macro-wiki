# Saad Sherida Al-Kaabi

Qatar's **Minister of State for Energy Affairs** and President & CEO of QatarEnergy. Operationally oversees Qatar's North Field gas development (the world's largest LNG-feedstock asset, geologically connected to Iran's [[SouthPars]]) and the Ras Laffan LNG liquefaction complex.

## Referenced in the wiki for
- **[[oil-jobs-bond-failure-and-other-fun]]** (6 March 2026): Al-Kaabi publicly warned that *"even if the war ended today, it would take Qatar weeks to months to get its delivery schedules back to normal"* and that the disruption could *"bring down the economies of the world."* This is the wiki's canonical source for the *"weeks to months to normalize"* framing that [[RoryJohnston]] and [[HFIResearch]] later quantify in more detail.
- **[[geopolitical-cousins-rory-johnston]]** (23 April 2026): Al-Kaabi's Qatari LNG infrastructure (Ras Laffan) was the target of Iran's reciprocal strike on 30 March 2026, matched against Israel's strike on Iran's [[SouthPars]]. The reciprocal exchange is the defining [[MutualAssuredDestructionEnergy]] moment per [[MarkoPapic]].
