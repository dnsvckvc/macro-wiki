# Kuppy (Harris Kupperman)

Hedge fund manager and macro commentator (Praetorian Capital / Kuppy's Event Driven Monitor). A friend of [[PauloMacro]], quoted in [[jumping-the-shark-again]] on the Iran conflict:

> "There's no way out of this, the only way out of Iran is through."

Paulo agrees on the game-theory logic (it's a [[NashEquilibrium]]) but calls the conclusion "a really really bad idea."
