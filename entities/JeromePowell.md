# Jerome Powell

Chair of the Federal Reserve. As of March 2026, in office through **May 2026**, when his term ends.

## Relevance in the wiki
Per [[PauloMacro]] in [[the-anatomy-of-a-crash]], the Powell lame-duck window is a systemic risk factor in the March–April 2026 crash setup: the Fed is "of two minds" on the oil crisis (per [[NeelKashkari]]), Powell doesn't leave until May, [[KevinWarsh]] (Trump's rumored successor pick) has not been confirmed, and the entire institution is in multi-month "institutional inertia" just as the market most needs a decisive backstop.
