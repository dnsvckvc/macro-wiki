# Scott Bessent

US Treasury Secretary as of the wiki's current date (April 2026).

## Where he shows up in the wiki
- Presumed to be "manning phones furiously" to manage foreign participation in the 30Y UST auction on 9 April 2026. ([[economic-data-copper-rollovers-and-more]])
- Rumored to be setting up a **Treasury PPT (Plunge Protection Team) trading desk** that would sell oil futures directly to suppress the price. Paulo in [[oil-jobs-bond-failure-and-other-fun]] and [[jumping-the-shark-when-a-taco-is-not-a-taco]]: "they know they have to deliver unless they intend to move the exchange goalposts and default via cash settlements and break the market." Paulo reads these interventions as part of the Trump jawboning toolkit that is *prolonging* the oil crisis by delaying demand destruction.

- **TGA rebuild as a liquidity drain** ([[liquidity-drains-redux]]): In 3Q25, Bessent rebuilt the TGA by ~$250bn. With the RRP at $78bn (effectively zero), the drain came from bank reserves. This triggered the 4Q25 Quant Quakes and the beginning of the [[RollingVARShock]]. Crypto fell first (the most liquidity-sensitive canary).
- **The 4.4% yield bogey (per [[macrovoices-528-luke-gromen]]):** Every time the US 10Y Treasury yield breaks **4.4%**, Bessent/Trump intervene. Pattern observed repeatedly over March–April 2026. After three ugly UST auctions in a row (including an ugly 2Y — "you should never have a bad 2Y auction"), Bessent executed a **$15B single-day Treasury buyback — the largest in history.** Luke Gromen: this is the clearest evidence of "shaping operations" in the Treasury market. Markets are being *managed*, not cleared.
- **TGA drawdown as a risk-asset support mechanism** ([[the-north-star-vs-liquidity]]): Bessent held back Treasury debt issuance ahead of April 15 tax receipts, drawing the TGA down ~$300bn from late 2025 (>$200bn since end of March alone). This injected liquidity into the banking system (bank reserves up $100bn+ in 2 weeks), creating the mechanical conditions for the late-March / early-April risk rally. Paulo treats this as deliberate — supporting risk assets ahead of tax season — and argues the tailwind reverses after April 15 when tax payments flow into the TGA.
