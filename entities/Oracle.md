# Oracle

Enterprise software and cloud infrastructure company. In the wiki, flagged by [[PauloMacro]] in [[jumping-the-shark-when-a-taco-is-not-a-taco]] as "the posterchild for a credit contagion downgrade from investment grade to high yield" and as a potential trigger for the equities [[TomHanksHasCovidMoment]] via its earnings report on 10 March 2026 — where it might use the oil crisis as cover to cut or delay [[AIInfrastructureInvestment]] capex without admitting the datacenter build-out is overextended.

## Update: 18% layoffs confirmed (per [[macrovoices-526-matt-barrie]])
[[MattBarrie]] in the 2 April 2026 MacroVoices interview confirms Oracle announced **18% of workforce cut** — validating Paulo's earlier flagging of Oracle as the credit-contagion posterchild. Oracle CDS had been ticking higher even as the stock bounced (per [[intensely-concerned-for-risk-with-a-catch]]).
