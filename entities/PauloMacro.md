# Paulo Macro

Independent macro trader and Substack writer, also known as **"Cloudbear"**. Cross-asset macro focus with deep energy and mining themes; also publishes proprietary frameworks for options-driven market microstructure and megacap rolling tops.

## Proprietary frameworks
- [[PreOpexMisdirectionWindow]] — dealer-gamma-driven Tue–Thu period ~9 days before monthly opex where trends can fake or reverse.
- [[RolloverSyndrome]] — the Nifty Fifty rolling-top pattern he uses to anticipate megacap reversals.
- [[AnatomyOfACrash]] — chart-pattern/sequencing framework for distinguishing a crash from a routine correction.
- [[TomHanksHasCovidMoment]] — archetype for the single visible event that converts private knowledge into [[CommonKnowledge]] and breaks market denial.
- [[NorthStar]] — the meta-navigational principle: see all conflict-era market action through the lens of Nash. Iran's strategy doesn't change; everything else is noise and timing.
- [[RollingBlowouts]] — macro framework from summer 2018; rolling ruptures across corners of the market always end in Risk Off.
- [[RollingVARShock]] — the micro version inside hedge-fund strategies: sequential blowups accelerating toward a convergence point.
- [[ShrubsRazor]] — "the funniest, most absurd outcome is also the most likely one." Coined in honor of [[LeShrub]].
- [[InvertAlwaysInvert]] reasoning ([[CharlieMunger]]) applied to [[CrackSpreads]] as a second-derivative read on crude.
