# Jim Bianco

Founder of **Bianco Research**, an institutional macro research firm. Primarily a bond/fixed-income specialist ("bond guy first, macro guy second"). Also manages the Bianco Research Total Return Index (tracked by WisdomTree ETF ticker WTBN). The wiki's **fifth author**.

## Key contributions to the wiki
- **Military-technical explanation** for why the [[StraitOfHormuz]] cannot be forced open: Iran's **Mosaic Doctrine** (sector commanders have standing orders to keep firing even if IRGC command is knocked out); decentralized drone/missile operations across mountainous terrain; the US military excels at offense but lacks a defensive shield. 39 days of bombing haven't stopped the attacks.
- **Ukraine drone-warfare revolution** as the template: 30–35K Russian casualties/month in 2026 (90% from drones); first war since the 19th century where drones are the primary method of killing. Ukraine has signed 10-year security agreements with Gulf states to bring interceptor drone tech, but scaling to 600 miles of the Strait takes time.
- **Nominal GDP framework** for the Fed's dilemma: nominal = real + inflation. If inflation exceeds the real-growth drag → rates higher. If real growth falls more → rates lower. Currently in the "rates higher" camp.
- **"3-ish percent inflation world"** — 5% fair-value rates, 7% mortgages as the new normal. Deglobalization and the end of free commerce on open seas as structural inflationary.
- **Permanent risk-premium repricing:** even with a deal, pre-Feb-28 levels are gone forever.
