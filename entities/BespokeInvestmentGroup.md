# Bespoke Investment Group

Independent market-research firm widely cited for historical-precedent and market-internal statistics.

## Wiki reference
Cited by [[KevinMuir]] in [[making-sense-of-the-last-two-months]] (19 April 2026) for a table of "this has never happened before" stats quantifying the exceptional character of the post-ceasefire rally. Muir uses the Bespoke data as grounds for worry rather than comfort: *"when a market behaves in a way that has never happened before, it worries me."*
