# Sal Mercogliano

Maritime historian and shipping-industry commentator (Campbell University; host of the *What's Going on With Shipping* YouTube channel). Frequent source of analysis on global seaborne trade, Suez/Panama/Hormuz chokepoints, and naval logistics.

## Referenced in the wiki for
Guest on [[KevinMuir]]'s *Watch Your Bids* podcast (cited in [[we-need-to-talk-about-interest-rates-and-oil]]) where he detailed the scale of damage to the global energy shipping complex after a month of [[StraitOfHormuz]] closure. Muir uses Mercogliano's analysis to argue that even a surprise diplomatic resolution would still leave **months** of physical damage to clear before shipping capacity recovers — hardening his view that elevated oil prices are "locked in" for some time.
