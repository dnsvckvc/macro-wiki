# Energy Aspects

London-based independent oil research consultancy founded by Amrita Sen. One of the most widely-cited buy-side energy-research shops, alongside [[HFIResearch]], Goldman, and [[RoryJohnston]]'s Commodity Context. Maintains proprietary balance-sheet and inventory models built on tanker/pipeline data.

## Referenced in the wiki for
Per [[HFIResearch]] in [[wctw-the-oil-market-breaking-point]] (citing Energy Aspects' "latest weekly perspective"):
- **Total supplies lost from the Iran crisis = ~930mmbbl.** This is the upper-end institutional estimate in the wiki's corpus.
- **Cumulative production lost May–December 2026 = ~340mmbbl.** The forward-curve air pocket quantified.
- Energy Aspects' estimates are *more aggressive* than HFI's — HFI explicitly concedes this, noting that their own storage-sensitivity analysis failed to factor in the 3–4 month restart lag for [[Iraq]] and [[Kuwait]]. Energy Aspects did factor it in.
