# Neel Kashkari

President of the Federal Reserve Bank of Minneapolis. Referenced in [[the-anatomy-of-a-crash]] via a WSJ article (linked by [[PauloMacro]]) in which Kashkari said the Fed "can sit tight as war clouds the outlook" and is "of two minds" on what to do about an oil crisis. Paulo uses this quote as evidence that the Fed has no clear framework for the inflationary-recession scenario and cannot reliably backstop an accident.
