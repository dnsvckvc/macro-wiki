# Rory Johnston

Founder of **Commodity Context** (commoditycontext.com). Oil-market analyst focused on fundamental supply/demand with monthly data reports on OPEC global balances and North American detailed balances. Publishes the weekly *Oil Context Weekly* (currently renamed *Oil and Iran War Context Weekly*). The wiki's **tenth author**.

- **Export-controls risk** — Jones Act waiver as precursor.
