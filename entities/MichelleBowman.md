# Michelle Bowman

Federal Reserve Board of Governors member. Through 2024–2025 one of the FOMC's dovish voices; repeatedly dissented in favor of rate cuts.

## March 17–18, 2026 FOMC inflection

Withdrew her dissent at the March 17–18 FOMC meeting alongside [[ChristopherWaller]]. Two of the three previously-dovish dissents disappeared in the same meeting — confirming the market's already-executed hawkish front-end repricing.

Per [[KevinMuir]] in [[why-did-the-front-end-get-hammered]]:
> *"Waller and Bowman (two of the three previous doves that had dissented on the unchanged monetary policy decision), rescinded their dissents, in essence, affirming the implied tightening."*
