# David Rosenberg

Canadian economist and founder of Rosenberg Research. Long-time bearish macro commentator known for recession calls and duration views.

## Referenced in the wiki for
[[KevinMuir]] in [[vix-inversion-buying-bonds-japan-jpm-option-whale]] notes that "even David Rosenberg has given up on the long-end" of the Treasury curve, choosing to focus on owning short-dated paper — Muir uses this as a sentiment-contrarian reason to think long-end bonds might "fly" from here.
