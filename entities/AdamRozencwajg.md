# Adam Rozencwajg

Co-founder of **Goehring & Rozencwajg**, a natural resource fund manager (partner Leigh Goehring started the strategy in 1991). They publish widely-cited commodity research that underpins their investment process. The wiki's **fourth author** and first physical-commodity/geology specialist.

## Where he fits in the wiki
Rozencwajg provides independent confirmation of several load-bearing wiki claims from a fundamentals/geology/depletion perspective — distinct from Paulo's trading-floor/microstructure lens, Muir's macro-fundamentals lens, and Howell's liquidity-cycle lens:
- **IEA surplus was fiction:** 2-3mmbpd surplus should have produced ~720mmbbl of inventory build in 2025. Inventories barely budged. OPEC surged 2-2.5mmbpd and it was absorbed. "The market was balanced."
- **Forward curve hasn't repriced:** spot ~$114, Dec26 only $75. The next shoe drops when people realize inventories aren't rebuilding.
- **Fertilizer/food is the classic asymmetry:** grain demand on a razor's edge; if yields falter because of fertilizer disruption, the market tightens "in a hurry." Confirms Paulo's [[Wheat]] thesis.
- **Gold:** oil will outperform for "a couple years" (the middle of a commodity bull). The *insolvency trade* for gold (1-3 years out) follows the current *debasement trade*.
- **Uranium:** supply deficit already here; Japanese stockpiles gone; $150/lb needed long-term.
