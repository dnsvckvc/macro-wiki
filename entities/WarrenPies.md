# Warren Pies

Co-founder of **3-Fourteen Research**. Equity-market research analyst publishing data-driven historical-precedent charts on social media.

## Wiki references
- In [[making-sense-of-the-last-two-months]] (19 April 2026), Muir cites Pies for the **only prior US instance of "S&P rallies 10% in 10 days into new all-time highs" = 21 March 2000** (dotcom final leg). "Narrator: *and that wasn't a good time to buy stocks.*" The April 2026 rally meets the criteria; Muir flags it as one of the "never happened before" stats that makes him worried.
- In [[outlining-the-bull-and-bear-arguments]] (20 April 2026), Muir cites a second Pies chart on the **bull side** — AI usage continuing to surprise bears. Used to articulate the "AI worries are overblown" bull pillar. The past year of US underperformance "might have just been a pause in the longer-term trend of US technological dominance."

Pies is the rare wiki source cited by Muir on both sides of the same question within 24 hours — once for a bearish historical precedent, once for a bullish secular trend.
