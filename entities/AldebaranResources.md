# Aldebaran Resources (ALDE.CN)

Copper junior mining company listed on the Canadian exchange (ticker ALDE.CN). Described by [[PauloMacro]] as his *largest copper junior* position. Currently conducting infill drilling to prepare a PFS (pre-feasibility study) targeted for late 2026.

## Recent data point
On 9 April 2026 Aldebaran reported a drill intercept of **0.62% Cu (0.66% CuEq) over nearly a kilometer** on infill drilling. Paulo flagged this as a "banger" because infill drilling (parallel/extension holes in already-drilled zones) rarely produces surprises. He views it as reinforcing ALDE's attractiveness as an M&A takeout target in the current [[Copper]] wave, though in the broader risk-off climate "nobody is paying attention."
