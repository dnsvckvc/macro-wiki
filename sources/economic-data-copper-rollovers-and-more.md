## Summary
[[PauloMacro]]'s 9 April 2026 note combines four threads: (1) markets are exiting the [[PreOpexMisdirectionWindow]], and a new high made in this window coupled with deteriorating internals ([[TRIN]] near 2) favours a reversal; (2) [[AldebaranResources]] (ALDE.CN) reported a ~1 km intercept of 0.62% Cu / 0.66% CuEq on infill drilling, under-appreciated by the market but reinforcing Paulo's copper-takeout thesis; (3) February PCE data shows the US consumer was already softening before the Iran/US/Israel war, confirming his "we were already going into a [[CreditCycle]] before the war" view, with CPI due the next day; (4) [[Amazon]] looks to be in an interesting [[RolloverSyndrome]] spot per his Nifty Fifty framework. He also notes 40 days since the Ayatollah's death (end of mourning) and a 30Y UST auction with [[ScottBessent]] likely working the phones.

## Key Claims
- We are exiting the [[PreOpexMisdirectionWindow]] — a Tue–Thu period ~9 days before monthly opex when dealer gamma/theta adjustments cause "cha-cha-cha" price action that can mask or even reverse the prevailing trend. Making a new high inside this window raises the odds of a reversal.
- Recent price action is distributive, not accumulative: [[TRIN]] near 2 (heavy volume in decliners vs. advancers) despite the rally is a warning.
- [[AldebaranResources]] (ALDE.CN) hit 0.62% Cu (0.66% CuEq) over ~1 km on infill drilling — a "banger" in a context where infill rarely surprises. Confidence growing in ALDE as an M&A takeout target in the current [[Copper]] wave.
- February PCE: headline in line but income and spend drifting lower. *The US consumer was already softening before the war broke out.*
- Paulo's thesis: the US was already entering a [[CreditCycle]] before the Iran/US/Israel war; the war compounds an existing pressure, it didn't start it.
- Inflationary pressures should not be expected to fade; CPI for March due the next day.
- [[Amazon]] is in an interesting spot in the [[RolloverSyndrome]] / Nifty Fifty framework.
- 40 days since the Ayatollah's death — end of Shia mourning period; successor rumored injured/incapacitated; expect policy announcements as path toward "peace" continues.
- 30Y UST auction scheduled; 10Y prior day was a "nothingburger"; [[ScottBessent]] is presumed to be actively managing foreign participation.
