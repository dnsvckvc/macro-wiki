## Summary
[[PauloMacro]]'s 27 March 2026 note argues that the market is grinding toward a [[TomHanksHasCovidMoment]] — the point at which the "We Can't Print Molecules" reality of a physical-world shock finally overwhelms financial asset pricing. He frames the Iran conflict as a [[NashEquilibrium]] where Iran's optimal strategy does not change regardless of Trump's moves (this was "Pearl Harbor" for Iran, requiring *Never Again* pain). He catalogs the escalating fragility: diminishing amplitude and wavelength of [[TrumpPumps]], the [[NachoTaco]] meme-cycle ([[LeShrub]]'s "Not Actually Changing Hormuz Opening"), and — critically — the [[JPMCollarTrade]] now sitting short-gamma against the 6475 SPX strike into the 31 March expiration, creating a potential 1987-style [[TurduckenOfMarketRisks]] as dealer delta-hedging, VIX calls, and 0DTE retail put-buying could compound. He notes [[MAG7]] is already in the crash pattern from his [[AnatomyOfACrash]] framework and is leading SPX lower.

## Key Claims
- Iran is in a [[NashEquilibrium]]: optimal strategy doesn't change regardless of what [[DonaldTrump]] does. Trump can withdraw, escalate, draft, put boots on the ground — *it does not matter*.
- "This was Pearl Harbor for Iran — *Never Again*." For the deterrent to hold, it has to genuinely hurt *everyone*: $200 oil, $8 gasoline, semi-fab force majeures, Nvidia at $50, SPX with a 4k handle.
- [[TrumpPumps]] are decaying in both amplitude and wavelength — reduced to 30 SPX handles in the thin 4:15pm aftermarket.
- The [[JPMCollarTrade]] (~tens of billions long S&P with a put-spread collar financed by a sold call) has its long-put leg at 6475 on the 31 March future. Dealers are short that strike; short gamma; low time; rising implied vol — a crash-ingredient setup.
- Compounding risk ingredients: VIX call chasing ("wingy vanna protection"), 0DTE retail put buying into an already-short-gamma dealer book — a potential **"Turducken of Market Risks"** with a 1987 flavour.
- The market is still waiting for its [[TomHanksHasCovidMoment]] in equities — a single event that creates [[CommonKnowledge]] that everyone sees that everyone else sees that equities are screwed. Most likely trigger: force majeures tied to [[MAG7]] and Hyperscalers, revealing the "perceived diversification" of a top-heavy S&P and Nasdaq.
- [[MAG7]] is already in the crash pattern described in Paulo's [[AnatomyOfACrash]] framework and is leading SPX lower.
- Quoted on dealer risk: [[KevinMuir]] to Paulo — "*you have to have some frickin' balls to be a dealer managing that position right now.*"
- [[Kuppy]] quoted: "*there's no way out of this, the only way out of Iran is through*" — Paulo agrees on the game-theory but calls it "a really really bad idea."
- The US is not militarily prepared for the coming conflict.
