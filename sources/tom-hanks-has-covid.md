## Summary
The foundational [[PauloMacro]] essay (5 March 2026, the wiki's earliest Paulo piece) that introduces the [[TomHanksHasCovidMoment]] archetype by retelling the February–March 2020 Covid sequence and mapping it onto the first week of the Iran/US/Israel war. Paulo draws a structural parallel: in 2020 he was short risk and screaming *"SELL EVERYTHING — THEY CANNOT PRINT TO STOP THIS"* while colleagues wanted to buy the -10% dip; today, tankers aren't moving but the market is shrugging it off because "nothing ever happens." The breaking news — **China tells top refiners to suspend diesel and gasoline exports** — is his candidate for the "Tom Hanks Has Covid" moment. The appendix explains *why* oil has seemed muted: US shale producers reflexively hedged the pop 3–6 months out on the strip, compressing forward prices and steepening backwardation. Paulo argues this "free option" hedging blows up if prices moon and collateral requirements spike — the same mechanic that caught silver producers in January when silver went $70 → $120.

## Key Claims
- **The 2020 common-knowledge template (told as personal experience):**
  - Feb 2020: Chinese cities shutting down, Italian hospitals filling up, face masks sold out — stocks at ATH, VIX at 15.
  - 3 March 2020: New Rochelle "community spread" → -50bps emergency Fed cut → Paulo on the floor: *"SELL F*CKING EVERYTHING — THEY CANNOT PRINT TO STOP THIS."*
  - ~11 March 2020: "Tom Hanks Has Covid" → common knowledge → markets collapsed → schools closed → everything changed.
- **The structural parallel (5 March 2026):**
  - Tankers are not moving through [[StraitOfHormuz]]. Iraq suffered a nationwide blackout. Qatar shut down LNG liquefaction (will take weeks to restart). But commentary is "donut, stocks down -1% NBD."
  - **10:18PM BREAKING: CHINA TELLS TOP REFINERS TO SUSPEND DIESEL, GASOLINE EXPORTS.** — Paulo's candidate for the "Tom Hanks Has Covid" moment.
  - This is not 4D chess: the world's second-largest economy with 3–4 months of crude in storage just told the world it's concerned this goes on for more than five days. "Snapcount" is about to kick off in energy as other nations follow with their own export bans.
- **Why oil seems muted — producer hedging mechanics:**
  - US shale producers reflexively hedged the pop 3–6 months forward (selling swaps and deferred contracts). This steepened the backwardation curve and muted the apparent price move.
  - A quarter of AEGIS Hedging Solutions' 350 oil-producer clients were on standby for Sunday's 6pm open with limit orders ready.
  - Post-June-2025 Iran bombing, the same hedging led to US production blowing out to 14mmbpd+ by fall — burst capacity to fill the hedges. But 13→14mmbpd of extra US production is nothing vs. 20mmbpd not transiting the Straits.
  - **The "free option" blows up if prices moon:** producers who hedged face margin calls. This was shown in silver ($70 → $120 in January) where producers were caught and Comex raised margins both sides. We will know who was "swimming naked" after Q1 results.
- **For decades people have cited "Middle East Geopolitical Risk" and "Hormuz Straits" as a risk factor, and now that it finally happens, they shrug it off because it never happened before.** *"I guess that's always how risk works — it leaps from the shadows, knife in hand, to cut everyone down to size, just when they think it no longer exists."*
